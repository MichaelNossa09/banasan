La Fundación Banasan es la entidad encargada de impulsar el desarrollo en las comunidades de influencia de la Comercializadora ubicadas en el departamento del Magdalena. Banasan Foundation

The Banasan Foundation is the entity in charge of promoting development in the communities of influence of the Comercializadora located in the department of Magdalena. Its strategy focuses on capacity building and empowerment of individuals and communities, enabling self-management and community social management.

The social management of our Foundation is framed in the following mission areas:

  • Social and Community Development

  • Income generation capacity

  • Culture and Environment
